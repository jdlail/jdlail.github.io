[![We Are Marshall](/images/wearemarshall-thumb.webp)](/images/wearemarshall.webp) Engine smoke forced a two-hour delay Friday in the charter jet flight of the [Marshall football team](https://www.bonesville.net/Articles/OtherArticles/Bonesville/Bonesville_Football/2006/111006_Marshall_plane.htm)to East Carolina. No big deal, except ... It happened on the game trip to Greenville where on Saturday East Carolina unveiled a plaque near the football stadium entrance honoring Marshall's 1970 team. The 1970 team died in a plan crash returning from an ECU game 36 years ago. That crash on Nov. 14, 1970, killed 75 people on a cold, rainy hillside in West Virginia. A movie, "[We Are Marshall](https://wearemarshall-themovie.warnerbros.com/)," about the crash and the aftermath comes out this fall. Whew, what a soundtrack.
