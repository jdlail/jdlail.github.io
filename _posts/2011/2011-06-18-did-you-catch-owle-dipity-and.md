Owle, [Dipity](https://www.dipity.com/ "Dipity") and Elph are not bands that did secret shows at the Bonnarroo festival a week ago in Manchester, Tenn. They are some of the tools that Knoxville News Sentinel visuals eidtor Jigsha Desai used to cover the massive music festival.  
  
She blogs about the [various tools she used and how they worked](https://blogs.knoxnews.com/roll/2011/06/covering-bonnaroo-2011-with-a.html).  
  
> As smartphones continue to innovate, we'll soon be covering all the news with a simple small device. Amazing times. And an amazing [Bonnaroo](https://www.bonnaroo.com/ "Bonnaroo Music Festival") experience.  
>
