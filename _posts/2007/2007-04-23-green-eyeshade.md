[Knoxnews](https://www.knoxnews.com) submitted entries are finalist in two online categories in the [Society of Professional Journalists'](https://www.spj.org) 57th annual Green Eyeshade Awards. The competition covers 11 southeastern states: Alabama, Arkansas, Florida, Georgia, Kentucky, Louisiana, Mississippi, North Carolina, South Carolina, Tennessee and West Virginia. The winners will be announced May 5 in Nashville. We hope we're happy! Our entries are up against some excellent journalism: **BEST USE OF MULTIMEDIA** • A Killer's Grip – Clarisa Gerlach, Tim Price, Tampa Tribune Staff, WFLA Staff, TBO.com, Tampa, Fla. • Carnival Center – Staff, The Miami Herald **• Sweet 15 – Jigsha Desai, Lauren Suhles, Kevin Cowan, Joe Howell, Knoxville (Tenn.) News Sentinel** **NICHE JOURNALISM** • Epic – Randy Johnson, Mark Caskie, Pace Communications **• GoVolsXtra – Online and Sports Departments, Knoxville (Tenn.) News Sentinel** • Tampa's Local Music Scene – Ryan Bauer, Tim Price, TBO.com, Tampa, Fla. Here's the [complete list of finalists](https://www.spj.org/news.asp?REF=669#669).
