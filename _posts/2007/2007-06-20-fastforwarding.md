Somebody put online video on fast forward. From a new survey: Daily vidoe usage up 56 percent over last year 14 percent of American 12 to 64 watch online video daily. 80 percent of 18 to 24s watch video at least once a week.

> News stories are reported by consumers as the most frequently viewed video they watch regularly on the Internet. Over a third of online Americans 12 to 64 watch online video news stories regularly.

\-- [Magid Media Futures news release](https://sev.prnewswire.com/advertising/20070618/NEM14518062007-1.html) While not broken down by demos, we also are seeing dramatic yeear-over-year growth in the number of video streams develivered from knoxnews via the AP video player.
