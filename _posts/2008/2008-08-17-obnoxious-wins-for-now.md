You may not like it (and I think it reduces traffic), but expect to see more, not less, [pre-roll video advertising.](https://gesterling.wordpress.com/2008/08/13/the-paradox-of-video-pre-roll/) We need to evolve to something that works for viewers and advertisers. Being obnoxious can't be good long term.
