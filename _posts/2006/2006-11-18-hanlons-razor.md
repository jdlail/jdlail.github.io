> Never attribute to malice that which can be adequately explained by stupidity.

Sometimes said as:

> Never assume malice when stupidity will suffice.

\-- attributed to Robert J. Hanlon in a collection of jokes about Murphy's Law. This is known as [Hanlon's razor](https://www.reference.com/browse/wiki/Hanlon's_razor).
