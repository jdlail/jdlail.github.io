The years other Internet companies were started.  

* [Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/) was founded in 1994.
* [Yahoo!](https://www.yahoo.com/) was founded in 1995.
* [eBay](https://ebay.com/ "eBay") was founded in 1995.
* [Craigslist](https://www.craigslist.org/ "Craigslist") was started in 1995 and incorporated in 1999.
* [Google](https://google.com/ "Google") was founded in 1998.
* [Flickr](https://www.flickr.com/) was founded in 2002.
* [MySpace](https://www.myspace.com/oxfam "MySpace") was founded in 2003.
* [Skype](https://www.skype.com/ "Skype") was founded in 2003
* [Facebook](https://facebook.com/ "Facebook") was founded in 2004.
* [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/ "YouTube") was founded in 2005.
* [Twitter](https://twitter.com/ "Twitter") was founded in 2006.
* [Fourquare.com](https://foursquare.com/) was founded in 2009
