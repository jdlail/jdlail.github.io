When you say it, it sounds like "I'm at knoxnews.com." And if you're not, you should be!

We're beginning to roll out a photo and video sharing site at [im.knoxnews.com](https://im.knoxnews.com). Upload a photo or video and let us know what you think. You can send us photos from the Web site, from a cell phone or by email. We're off to a good start of submissions, but we need yours.

The site is powered by Nashville-based [Cell Jounalist Inc.](https://www.celljournalist.com/), whose platform is also being used by the [Commercial Appeal](https://focus.commercialappeal.com/) in Memphis and [WKRN](https://www.nashflix.com/), a Nashville TV station.
