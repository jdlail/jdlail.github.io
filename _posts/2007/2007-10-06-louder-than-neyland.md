State at the 1984 game in Raleigh.](/images/1006goalposts-thumb-150x219.webp)](/images/1006goalposts.webp) Now, East Carolina football fans (where I went to college) have had a rep for being a tad bit on the rowdy side. There have been those four little incidents ([see photo caption](https://www.ecu.edu/cs-admin/mktg/east/Sports-Fall-2007.cfm)), among others, at N.C. State.  
  
But louder than Vol fans in Neyland?  
  
ECU athletic director Terry Holland includes this quote in an [email](https://link.paciolan.com/viewmsg.cfm?n35108s366c11544t364) to ECU supporters:  
  
_"To be honest, I'm not so sure that 43,000 wasn't louder than that 107,000," Southern Miss defensive coordinator Jay Hopson said. "I was like, 'Goolllyy!' That was a loud crowd that rocked for 60 minutes."_
