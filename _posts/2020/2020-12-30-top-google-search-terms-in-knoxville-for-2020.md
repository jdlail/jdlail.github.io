The [top 25 rising Google search terms for the Knoxville area in 2020](https://trends.google.com/trends/explore/RELATED_QUERIES/1609346400?hl=en-US&tz=300&geo=US-TN-557&sni=3) are not much of a surprise -- almost of them had to do with "Life in the Pandemic" or the election. Popular Google Doodle Games, Kobe Bryant, Tesla stock and PS5 may be the exceptions.

  1. coronavirus Breakout
  2. stimulus check Breakout
  3. coronavirus tennessee Breakout
  4. election results Breakout
  5. 2020 election results Breakout
  6. coronavirus update Breakout

  18. ps5 1100%
  19. unemployment 1000%
  20. weather tomorrow 950%
  21. tn unemployment 950%
  22. jobs4tn 850%
  23. cdc 600%
  24. zoom 450%
  25. tesla stock 350%

The [results for the state of Tennesse](https://trends.google.com/trends/explore?geo=US-TN#RELATED_QUERIES) were similar, but include George Floyd, Joe Biden and Kim Jong Un. Tesla stock did not make the state top 25.

Google defines "rising" as "queries with the biggest increase in search frequency since the last time period. Results marked "Breakout" had a tremendous increase, probably because these queries are new and had few (if any) prior searches."
