As newspapers try to reinvent themselves and find new models for success, here's a thought:  
  
> Essentially, all models are wrong, but some are useful.  
>
> \- [George E.P. Box](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/George_Box)  
>

That's worth remembering. We can't wait for the perfect model, just some useful ones.  
  
(via [Alexander Kjerulf](https://positivesharing.com/2008/07/quote-116/))
