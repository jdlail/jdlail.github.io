Dr. [Helen Smith](https://drhelen.blogspot.com/), sometimes known as the "Insta-Wife," has made her [Pajamas TV debut](https://pjtv.com/?cmd=video&video-id=676&media-id=1931). Smith and her husband, [Instapundit](https://www.instapundit.com/) blogger Glenn Reynolds, are doing Internet TV or video from their basement. It's not that different from what you see on the cable news networks, another sign that the next great media empire may be headquartered on a laptop.
