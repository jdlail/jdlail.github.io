![Media Innovation Awards](/images/mediainnovationawards.webp)[Knoxnews](https://www.knoxnews.com/) won three Digital Edge Awards on Monday from the Newspaper Association of America. The Digital Edge Awards are part of the NAA's larger Media Innovation Awards. Winning was good!  
  
The Digital Edge Awards, or Edgies, are one of the most prestigious online newspapers awards. The Knoxville News Sentinel competes in a category with a large number of papers, those with print circulations between 75,000 and 250,000. There are many excellent newspaper Web sites in this group and lots of innovative things happening.  
  
We won:  
  
Best Overall News Site  
  
Most Innovative Visitor Participation ([schoolmatters.knoxnews.com](https://schoolmatters.knoxnews.com/))  
  
Best Design and Site Architecture.  
  
Here is a [complete list](https://www.naa.org/blog/digitaledge/1/2008/02/NAA-Announces-Digital-Edge-Award-Winners.cfm) and [details](https://www.naa.org/docs/Digital-Edge/MedIAs_de2008awards.pdf).
