Can't beat this deal!

> Journalists, journalism educators and journalism students from diverse backgrounds are invited to apply for a diversity scholarship to attend the [Murfreesboro NewsTrain](https://www.apme.com/?page=Murfreesboro).
>
> Successful applicants for these competitive awards will have their registration fee waived; they must pay their own travel expenses.
>
> **Apply by Aug. 25** by [answering these questions](https://goo.gl/forms/Q6qUWmkURyzsY8m12) and [emailing](mailto:val.hoeppner@mtsu.edu) your resume and up to three work samples and/or class assignments to Val Hoeppner, director of MTSU's Center for Innovation in Media. Journalism educators need not submit work samples.

The NewsTrain is Sept. 30-Oct. 1, 2016.
