Jeff Herr, Director Of Interactive Media, Lee Enterprises, does a presentation called "Get Your Mojo On!" His talk is broken into [eight videos](https://www.youtube.com/view_play_list?p=57681ED92E508A9A).  
  
His is another presentation from the "Smart Phones for Smart Journalists" wrokshop in Nashville on April 9. The one-day workshop was sponsored by the [Online News Association](https://www.journalists.org/ "Online News Association") and the [Freedom Forum](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Freedom_Forum "Freedom Forum") Diversity Institute. The workshop was underwritten in part by Cell Journalist, a Nashville company with a photo and video platfrom used by hundreds of media Web sites, and the Scripps Howard Foundation.
