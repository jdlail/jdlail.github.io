![Lauren Spuhler/Bonnaroo](/images/lauren_spuhler-2006_bonnaroo.webp)Three-time Bonnaroo survivor (see [2007](https://blogs.knoxnews.com/knx/bonnaroo2007/), [2006](https://blogs.knoxnews.com/knx/spuhler/), [2005](https://blogs.knoxnews.com/knx/spuhler/archives/2005/06/) coverage) and online producer [Lauren Spuhler](https://www.laurenspuhler.com/) is getting in the 'Roo mood with [previews of Knoxville bands](https://blogs.knoxnews.com/knx/bonnaroo2008/) scheduled to play at Bonnaroo in Tennessee.  
  
Spuhler will be Manchester-bound mid-week with music writer Wayne Bledsoe. Follow her [blog reports](https://blogs.knoxnews.com/knx/bonnaroo2008/), which will include photo and vidoes, as well as on [Twitter](https://twitter.com/bonnaroo). (Follow [twitter.com/bonnaroo](https://twitter.com/bonnaroo)!)
