Maybe so obvious, is it not worth noting, but I will: The capture Col. Muammar Gaddafi [was caught on video](https://www.globalpost.com/dispatches/globalpost-blogs/the-casbah/gaddafi-dead-video-initial-capture-exclusive?fb_comment_id=fbc_10150366005543234_19872567_10150366286653234#f12d63d838) and that the video was shot with an iPhone by a participant; not a journalist.  
  
In photos and videos of his death and body, nearly everyone seems to [have a camera phone](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jQWPnC74JqM&feature=youtu.be).  
  
[The first rough draft of history](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Phil_Graham) has become raw video shot with a cell phone capable of shooting video and with the capability of being quickly shared and posted on the Internet.  
  
As I write this, [Google Plus](https://plus.google.com/118366622035212955924/posts/7w1FCCyxgoo) and [Twitter](https://twitter.com/#%21/__kashif__/status/128110849587036160) and [news websites](https://www.reuters.com/video/2011/10/22/earthquake-hits-eastern-turkey?videoId=223818925&newsChannel=newsOne) are posting raw video from an earthequake in a fairly remote region of Turkey.
