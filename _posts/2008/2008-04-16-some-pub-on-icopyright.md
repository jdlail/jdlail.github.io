![icopyright](/images/icopyright.webp)iCopyright has been getting some pub this week with the announcement of a deal with AP.  
  
We've been using [iCopyright](https://www.icopyright.com/) or its technology for a several years on [knoxnews](https://www.knoxnews.com/) and I've always liked how it works. When i first saw it, it was like, wow, it's basically all self-service? And in most cases, it is.  
  
It's not meant as a way to keep grandma from emailing an article to Aunt Suzie, but rather a way to efficiently manage commercial reuse. And in these days of dwindling resources at newspapers, that's more important than ever.
