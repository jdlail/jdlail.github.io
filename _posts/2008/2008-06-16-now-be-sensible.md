With the decision to mothball [OJR.org](https://www.ojr.org/), long-time "online guy" and former Scripps colleague Robert Niles has launched a new site: [sensibletalk.com](https://www.sensibletalk.com/)  
  
> Why "sensible talk"?  
>
> Because that's how we make better journalism -- with sensible talk. Our ability to report is only as good as our ability to perceive, and our perceptions are best informed by both our senses and our understanding of facts.  
>

There's [more](https://www.sensibletalk.com/).
